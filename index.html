Motivation

We are inspired by the recent news that the US is imposing tariffs on foreign import and the potential trade war between the US and China.
We would like to explore the import and export of the US with other countries in the world over the year and draw insights about the US trade dynamics across time.

Overview
US International Trade

Both imports and exports increase exponentially over the years. Trade deficit, as represented by the inverted bar chart on the left, increases at the same time.

Exports by Product Type

The stacked bar chart shows that machinery & transportation equipment takes a major share of US exports for each year, followed by chemicals and manufactured goods.

Use the dropdown menu to select and view the exports trend for the product type you are interested in.

US Export by State

California and Texas the the largest number of exports as indicated by the darker color.

Hover on individual states for their top 3 exports respectively.

Conclusion

Given the increasingly exploding amount of trade deficits that the US has with the rest of the world,
as well as the apparent interdependencies among countries,
let us better not declare any trade war to make everyone's life easier:)
